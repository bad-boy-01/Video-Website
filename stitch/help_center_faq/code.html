Coin Purchases & Billing

How do I buy more coins? expand_more
You can purchase coins by clicking the "+" icon next to your balance in the top header. We support credit cards, PayPal, and local payment methods depending on your region. All transactions are securely processed.
My coin purchase is not showing up expand_more
Most purchases reflect instantly. If yours hasn't appeared after 15 minutes, please try refreshing your app or checking your email for a receipt. If the issue persists, contact billing support with your transaction ID.

Video Access & Playback

Can I watch videos offline? expand_more
Currently, offline viewing is only available for premium subscribers on mobile devices. Look for the download icon on the video detail page.
Why is the video quality low? expand_more
VideoStream automatically adjusts quality based on your internet speed. You can manually select 1080p or 4K in the player settings (the gear icon) if your connection supports it.

Account Security

How do I reset my password? expand_more
Go to the login screen and click "Forgot Password." We'll send a reset link to your registered email address. For security reasons, the link expires after 2 hours.
Enabling Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) expand_more
Navigate to Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication. You can use SMS or an authenticator app like Google Authenticator or Authy to secure your account.

Technical Support

Supported browsers and devices expand_more
VideoStream is compatible with the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We also have dedicated apps for iOS, Android, and Smart TVs (Samsung, LG, and Android TV).
Clear cache and cookies expand_more
If the player isn't loading, try clearing your browser's cache and cookies. This resolves 90% of player-related issues. If it still doesn't work, disable any ad-blocking extensions.

Still need help?

Our support team is available 24/7 to assist you with any questions or technical difficulties.
